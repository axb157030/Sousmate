About

Sousmate is a kitchen aid, helping clients get the food they want delivered to their doors. You can upload their favorite recipes, which Sousmate will display for users cooking that recipe in a step-by-step fashion. Sousmate will track the cooking time for foods, and will alert users when certain periods of time have elapsed. Under your discretion sousmate will order that food to be delivered to you.
