About

Sousmate is a kitchen aid, helping you get the food you want delivered to your doors. You can upload your favorite recipes, which Sousmate will display for you cooking that recipe in a step-by-step fashion. Sousmate will track the cooking time for foods, and will alert you when certain periods of time have elapsed. Under your discretion sousmate will order that food to be delivered to you.
